What's the Fuss?

Unbiased Break is more than a news app; it is meant to revolutionize politics. How are we planning to do this?

Phase 1: News & Social Media

This is the current state of the app. You can view news articles geared toward your political party of choice. If you want to compare differing viewpoints, simply toggle between parties within the same topic. Additionally, you can comment on articles and reply to threads. Don't forget to bookmark your favorite news stories for future reference.

Phase 2: Voter and Political Data

Users should expect to have a swift and clear overview of their political representation. While this phase might not be revolutionary, it is a necessary step on our journey to Phase 3.

Phase 3: Politicians and Voter Certified

Phase 3 will provide the world with what we have coined "Voter Certified". More details of this phase will be revealed as we get closer to its release.
